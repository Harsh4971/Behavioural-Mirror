─── DETAILED DESCRIPTION ──────────────────────────────────────

Mirror turns your Google Meet calls into behavioral data — and behavioral data into coaching.

After every meeting, you'll see exactly how you showed up: how much you spoke, how fast, how many filler words you used, when you interrupted, how long you waited before responding, whether you asked questions, and whether your energy rose or fell. Then the AI builds a behavioral profile across every call, so the coaching gets sharper every session.


HOW IT WORKS

→ Open Mirror's side panel during any Google Meet call
→ Click Record — Mirror captures audio directly from your browser tab, no installs, no extras
→ Stop recording when the meeting ends (or any time you want)
→ Mirror transcribes, diarizes, and analyzes the audio in under two minutes
→ Read your insights in the panel while the meeting is still fresh


WHAT MIRROR ANALYZES

Talk ratio — how much of the conversation was yours, broken into you / others / silence

Speech rate — words per minute, and whether you accelerated or slowed through the session

Filler words — rate per 100 words, and whether the pattern is random or clustered

Interruptions — both given and received

Response latency — how long you wait before speaking after someone else finishes

Vocabulary richness — lexical diversity across your turns

Questions asked — count and pattern

Vocal energy — whether your energy rose, held, or declined through the call

Monologue length — your longest unbroken turn

...and more


BEHAVIORAL DIMENSIONS

Each session is scored across three groups:

Emotional State — confidence, nervousness, emotional intensity, topic comfort, enthusiasm

Relational Dynamics — rapport, power balance, empathy, conversational respect, mutual engagement

Communication Effectiveness — clarity, assertiveness, listening quality, persuasiveness, adaptability

Conversation Arc — who drove the session, tension level, how it opened vs. closed, whether there was a turning point


YOUR MIRROR PROFILE

Mirror builds a behavioral profile that grows with every session:

• Context detection: Mirror auto-identifies the type of conversation (evaluative, collaborative, influential, negotiation, adversarial, supportive, and more) and applies the right benchmarks
• Cross-session insights: The Mirror Feed surfaces patterns the data keeps seeing across calls — things you'd never notice from a single session
• Trend tracking: See how your filler rate, talk ratio, and speech rate are moving over time
• Personality synthesis: An AI summary of your communication style, updated session by session
• Blind spots: Conversation contexts you haven't recorded yet, so you know where your mirror is incomplete
• Recurring coaching: The areas that keep appearing across sessions, ranked by frequency


PERSONALIZED COACHING

After every session, Mirror generates ranked coaching suggestions grounded in what the transcript actually showed — not generic advice. You can tell Mirror whether each observation resonated with you (yes / somewhat / no), and future insights are calibrated to avoid noise and focus on what's real for you.


PRIVACY — YOUR AUDIO IS NEVER STORED

Audio is written to temporary processing storage only during analysis, then permanently deleted the moment results are ready. Mirror never stores, retains, or sells your recordings.

What is stored (in your account): transcripts, analysis results, behavioral scores, and insights. You own your data and can delete everything at any time from the extension.

See the full privacy policy at mirrorai.live/privacy


VOICE ENROLLMENT (OPTIONAL)

For uploaded recordings with multiple speakers, Mirror can identify which voice is yours using a voice embedding trained from a 30-second sample. This is entirely optional, skippable, and deletable. The embedding is a mathematical representation — not a recording.


USAGE

• 15 analysis sessions per month (free)
• Works on Google Meet only (side panel auto-activates on meet.google.com)
• Audio files must be under 20 minutes
• You can also upload recordings directly from the Upload tab (Zoom, phone calls, in-person recordings)


GET STARTED

1. Install Mirror and open any Google Meet call
2. Click the Mirror icon in your toolbar to open the side panel
3. Complete a 30-second voice training (optional but recommended)
4. Click Record
5. After the call, click Stop and wait ~90 seconds for your analysis
